Help & Support

Welcome to the Local Community Event Portal! This page provides answers to common questions and guidance on using the portal.

Getting Started

  • Browse upcoming events on the Events page.
  • Use filters to narrow down events by category or location.
  • Register for events through the Register page.

Features

  • Gallery: View photos from past events to get a feel for our community.
  • Promo Videos: Watch promotional videos to stay updated on exciting happenings.
  • Nearby Events: Find events happening near your location with the geolocation feature.
  • Preferences: Save your favorite event types to personalize your experience.

Troubleshooting

  • If the geolocation feature isn't working, please allow location permissions in your browser settings.
  • If videos don't play, ensure your browser supports HTML5 video.
  • Clear your preferences using the button on the Contact page if you experience display issues.

Contact Us

If you have further questions or need assistance, please reach out at support@localcommunityevents.org.
